Tmux scroll up at mac terminal

at mac terminal,
when you at tmux,
cant use scroll up and down to watch scrollback buffer

How to fix ?
add this line to ~/.tmux.conf

set -g terminal-overrides 'xterm*:smcup@:rmcup@'
